How we work

Projects

madeMocal is a white-box Location-Based Marketing system which can evolve through a collaboration between the client and the development team. The mapping and routing engine of Mocal provides the ability to identify customers near your premises, direct them in-store and have the customer save and share information.

We, at Mocal Technologies, promote adaptive planning, evolutionary development, early delivery, continuous improvement, and encourages rapid and flexible response to change.

Mocal can be your path-2-purchase location-based personal assistance, designed around store locations and partner services. Location Based Marketing can be built into an existing client app or a new customer loyalty program designed to feed customers and business your way.
