Motormouth

The MotorMouth Team takes pride in ensuring MotorMouth is the premier source of fuel prices for motorists.

How does it work? Fuel prices are currently reported for Brisbane, the Gold and Sunshine Coasts, Sydney, Melbourne, Hobart, Adelaide, Canberra, Darwin and Perth. MotorMouth collects fuel prices daily in these areas (excluding public holidays). The majority of prices are reported at 8:45am and 4:45pm local time. Additional prices are also displayed between 6am and 3pm. For more information, please refer to Motormouth.com.au.
