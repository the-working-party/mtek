About CHAINFORMATION

CHAINFORMATION [CCMTM] is dynamic, scalable and designed to satisfy the information needs of all franchise and multi-store operations.

Today a franchise intranet is regarded as a necessity, as new social platforms and mobile opportunities are prioritised.

The majority of the functionality, structure and graphical appearance can be fully customized, and the platform offers a wide array of add-on modules, providing smart solutions based on your business needs. The ability to mirror the organizational structure to enable sharing of trackable information to targeted franchisees and their employees, is the core of the CCMTM platform. Client created information, from short weekly news and downloadable files, to complete franchise manuals and a whole database of unit info, is gathered in one place.

Fast, easy and usable. And affordable!
